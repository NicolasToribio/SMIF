Alfred University Investment Competition

Join our prestigious investment competition and showcase your financial acumen!

Competition Overview

The Alfred University Investment Competition is an exciting opportunity for students to apply their investment knowledge and skills in a real-world scenario. Participants will compete in teams to develop the best stock pitches, with the chance to win amazing prizes and gain invaluable experience.

This event is open to all students, regardless of their major or level of experience in finance. We encourage everyone to participate and take advantage of this unique learning opportunity.

Competition Details

  • Date: April 15, 2024
  • Location: Alfred University, Saxon Drive, Alfred, NY
  • Teams: 3-5 members per team
  • Prizes: 1st place: $5,000, 2nd place: $3,000, 3rd place: $1,000

To participate, please register your team by clicking the "Register Now" button below. We look forward to seeing you at the event!
