About Us

A unique feature of the finance major at Alfred University is the opportunity to gain real-world experience with our Student Managed Investment Fund (SMIF).

Our students actively manage a real stock portfolio, applying their classroom knowledge to real-world situations. This hands-on experience is invaluable in preparing for careers in finance.

In October 1998, David Eccles School of Business finance students joined the D.A. Davidson & Co. Student Investment Fund program, which allows students to invest $50,000 in a working stock portfolio...
